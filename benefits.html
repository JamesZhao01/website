Benefits

Benefits to Fencing:

→ Improved Eye-Hand Coordination

→ More Self-Confidence

→ Improves Quickness and Agility

→ Mentally Challenging

→ Very helpful to get into college. Many students use fencing as an effective tool to show uniqueness, and ability to think on their feet that Universities appreciate.

→ Excellent and fun way to get in shape at any age

Fencing is Fun!

Most students start fencing between 8-14 years of age but there are exceptions to everything. Currently I have a 70 year old student who always wanted to fence and says that it is a great physical workout with a mental aspect necessary to remember all the different moves.
